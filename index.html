About us

Wellness Diced is a community of those who are committed to Life-long, Life-Friendly & healthy habits based on a coaching relationship by providing guidance and resources on nutrition, fitness, self-care and mental health.

Our Vision:


Our vision is to create a culture of health and wellness that inspires and motivates individuals to prioritize their physical and mental well-being to make lasting lifestyle changes that positively impact their lives and the lives of those around them , ultimately touching the lives of 1 million people.


Our Mission:


Our mission is to inspire a culture of healthy living by helping people to overcome lifestyle disorders. We aim to foster a community of like-minded individuals who are committed to living their best lives, embracing wellness as a journey rather than a destination.

Transformation Journies we Provide

01.

OVERWEIGHT

Obesity increases the risk of several health problems including heart disease, stroke, diabetes, and certain types of cancer. In India, more than 135 million people are affected by obesity.

02.

Underweight

Underweight is a concern as it increases the risk of malnutrition, weakened immunity, and developmental issues. In India, around 20% of adults are underweight, and globally, underweight contributes to 3.7 million deaths annually

03.

Diabeties

Approximately 1 out of 5 people in India have diabetes. Diabetes is a chronic disease that affects the body's ability to produce or use insulin, leading to high blood sugar levels. If left untreated, it can cause serious health complications such as heart disease, kidney failure, and blindness.

04.

Fatty Liver

Fatty liver, a condition where excess fat accumulates in the liver, affects up to 25% of people globally. It can lead to liver inflammation, scarring, and eventually liver failure, making it a serious health concern that requires attention and treatment.

05.

Skin & Hair Care

Every 3 in 5 individuals have a skin concern. Taking care of your skin is essential as it is the largest organ in the body and protects us from external factors. Neglecting skincare can lead to premature aging, skin damage, and even skin cancer.
In India, over 60% of people experience some form of hair loss, with factors such as stress, genetics, and diet contributing to the problem. Excessive hair fall can lead to self-esteem issues and may also be a symptom of an underlying health condition.

06.

Chemical free LifeStyle

Creating a chemical-free home is essential for maintaining a healthy and sustainable lifestyle. By avoiding harmful chemicals in household products, such as cleaning supplies, air fresheners, and personal care items, we can reduce exposure to toxins and promote overall well-being.

07.

PCOD/PCOS

Polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS) affects 1 in 10 women of childbearing age, causing hormonal imbalances, irregular periods, and infertility. It also increases the risk of type 2 diabetes, heart disease, and depression

08.

Kids Care

Childhood health issues are a growing concern worldwide. In India, 1 in 4 children is malnourished, while 10-20% suffer from mental health problems. Childhood obesity has also tripled in the last decade, increasing the risk of chronic diseases later in life.
